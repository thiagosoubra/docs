About the Author

Sandie Gustus B.A., Dip. Ed., Chart. PR., volunteered with the London Educational Center of the International Academy of Consciousness (IAC) from 2001 to 2014. During this time, she taught in the UK, Netherlands and Finland, and published numerous articles on the Out-of-Body Experience (OBE) and related phenomena in the UK and internationally.

In 2014 she left IAC to further assist with the internationalization of conscientiology, and currently volunteers with the Interassistential Services for the Internationalization of Conscientiology (ISIC), a Brazil-based organization.

Born in Australia, Sandie lives in Paris, France, where she is the global Head of Internal Communication for a French multinational.
